Zerno coffeeshop

Zerno is a small coffeeshop in the centre of Minsk. We are focused on making the best coffee possible. We use only freshly brewed high quality arabica for our coffee. Check in to try our espresso,cappuchino, flat white, latte and a bunch of other choices including our great BREW BAR varieties.
